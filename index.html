Our Story

Made with
care & intention.

Teduh began as a dream of making specialty coffee accessible — not intimidating. We wanted a place that felt like home, where the coffee is honest and the atmosphere is warm.


Today, Teduh is a multi-channel coffee brand rooted in Muar. We roast our own beans, run a café, supply to wholesale clients, and pack drip bags for coffee lovers to enjoy anywhere.

What Drives Us

Our Values

01
Craft
Every roast, every brew, every cup is treated as an opportunity to do something well. No shortcuts, no compromises.
02
Community
We're a place for Muar — a gathering point for good people, good conversation, and good coffee.
03
Calm
Teduh means shelter. We want everyone who visits to feel unhurried — comfortable to stay, slow down, and breathe.
