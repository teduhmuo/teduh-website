Community

More than coffee —
a place to gather.


Teduh is not only a coffee shop. It is a meeting point for creatives, skaters, musicians, artists, and coffee lovers in Muar.


Through events, collaborations, and open gatherings, we hope Teduh becomes a small cultural hub where people connect, share stories, and build something meaningful together — whether it’s music, art, friendships, or simply moments that make the city feel more alive.

Our Story

Made with
care & intention.

Teduh began in 2020 as a small family effort between me, my wife, and our son. What started as a simple idea slowly grew through long days, trial and error, and many difficult moments along the way.

The journey was never easy. We started small, working through the uncertainty of the MCO period, facing challenges like floods that affected the shop, and learning everything step by step. From setting up a small mobile coffee setup by the roadside to joining events like Pesta Kopi 2023, Muar Uptown Raya 2023 and Hin On The Move 2024, every experience became part of how Teduh grew.

Along the way we learned many things — including roasting our own coffee beans, improving our craft, and slowly building something we could truly call our own.

Today, Teduh has grown into a proper coffee shop with a small team that helps carry the journey forward. What remains the same is the intention behind it all: to build something meaningful not only for our family, but also for the staff who grow with us and for the community in Muar and the surrounding areas.

We hope this journey continues — slowly, patiently — while giving the best we can to the people who support us.

Sincerely,
Akmal
